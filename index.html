YouTube


Triggers:
When you open up the app, this microinteraction plays
Rules:
When the app opens, the YouTube logo appears and then the red box turns flattens and stretches into a video progress bar. Simultaneously, the arrow shifts left of the bar and a red line comes along and fills up the bar. Once the red has filled up the bar, it shrinks into the arrow as the arrow moves right towards the center
Feedback:
When you open up the app, this microinteraction plays
Loops & Modes:
There are no loops or modes
